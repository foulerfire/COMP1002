About Atari, Inc.

Atari, Inc. is a pioneer in electronic entertainment, creating innovative experiences for arcades and the home. Our goal is to bring exciting new technology into everyday life.

Our mission

To deliver arcade-style entertainment for the modern home through reliable hardware, quality games, and a growing library of interactive experiences.

A brief history

  • 1972: Atari is founded in California.
  • 1972: Pong introduces video gaming to arcades.
  • 1975: Home Pong brings electronic play to living rooms.
  • 1977: The Atari VCS launches with interchangeable cartridges.
