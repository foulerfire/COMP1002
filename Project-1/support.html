Getting started

  1. Connect the TV/game switch to your television antenna input.
  2. Plug the switch into the Atari VCS and connect the power adapter.
  3. Insert a game cartridge firmly into the cartridge slot.
  4. Turn on your television and set it to channel 2 or 3.
  5. Turn the Atari power switch to ON.

Troubleshooting

No picture

  • Confirm the TV is set to channel 2 or 3.
  • Check that the TV/game switch is securely connected.
  • Power off the console, re-seat the cartridge, and try again.

No sound

  • Check your television volume and mute settings.
  • Try a different cartridge to confirm the system is running.

Controls not responding

  • Make sure the controller is fully plugged into the correct port.
  • Try switching controller ports.
  • Inspect the cable for damage.
